# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project respectful, inclusive, and harassment-free for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still prioritizing safety and privacy.
- Keep discussions focused on authorized, ethical automation and Android development.
- Redact secrets, personal data, private package names, device identifiers, and sensitive screenshots.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Requests or contributions intended to bypass rules, misrepresent user actions, or automate services without permission.
- Sharing credentials, private deployment details, personal data, or doxxing material.
- Repeated off-topic or abusive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict participation for behavior that violates this Code of Conduct. Serious safety or privacy concerns may be handled privately.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to the repository maintainers through the repository owner's preferred private channel. Do not include secrets or personal data beyond what is necessary to understand the report.
